Greetings & Salutations

I'm excited to be teaching at MICA and look forward to the opportunity to share this creative, challenging field with you.

In addition to my role as an adjunct professor at MICA, I am also the lead front-end developer at Lumen Learning, where we use evidence-based practices to build tools to enable unprecedented learning for all students.

Prior to joining Lumen Learning, I developed web sites as a freelancer, in collaboration with Left Bank Creative, as senior front-end developer for Detroit Trading and as senior UX engineer at Fractured Atlas. Before making the transition to tech, I worked at several art museums, including serving as the von Hess Graduate Fellow at the Baltimore Museum of Art. I received a BA in journalism from Indiana University of Pennsylvania, where I attended the Cook Honors College, and also studied at Sheffield-Hallam University in the History of Art, Design and Film program and graduate studies in Museum Communication at University of the Arts.
