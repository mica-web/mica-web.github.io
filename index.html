Greetings & Salutations

I'm excited to be teaching at MICA and look forward to the opportunity to share this creative, challenging field with you.

In addition to my role as an adjunct professor at MICA, I am also an engineering manager at Panorama Education.

Prior to joining Panorama Education, I developed web sites as a freelancer, in collaboration with Left Bank Creative, and as a front-end lead for Detroit Trading, Fractured Atlas, and Lumen Learning. Before making the transition to tech, I worked at several art museums, including serving as the von Hess Graduate Fellow at the Baltimore Museum of Art. I received a BA in journalism from Indiana University of Pennsylvania, where I attended the Cook Honors College, and also studied at Sheffield-Hallam University in the History of Art, Design and Film program and graduate studies in Museum Communication at University of the Arts.
