--- title: About ---

Greetings & Salutations!

I'm excited to be teaching Front End Web Development and look forward to the opportunity to share this creative, challenging field with you.

In addition to my role as an adjunct professor at MICA, I am also the senior UX engineer at Fractured Atlas, where I lead a small but mighty UX team crafting ever-better experiences for the artists and art organizations who use our products and the people who support them.

Prior to joining Fractured Atlas, I developed web sites as a freelancer, in collaboration with Left Bank Creative and as senior front-end developer for Detroit Trading. Before making the transition to tech, I worked at several art museums, including serving as the von Hess Graduate Fellow at the Baltimore Museum of Art. I received a BA in journalism from Indiana University of Pennsylvania, where I attended the Cook Honors College, and also studied at Sheffield-Hallam University in the History of Art, Design and Film program and graduate studies in Museum Communication at University of the Arts.

You can learn more about me via my resume or check out some of my projects on GitHub.
