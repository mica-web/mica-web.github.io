Project 1: Resume

In Brief

Use your current knowledge of HTML and CSS to markup and format your resume.

Purpose

  • Demonstrate your understanding of basic HTML and CSS.
  • Reinterpret your resume based on the priority guide reading.

Process

  1. Do the week 01 reading and follow the instructions to create a priority guide for your resume.
  2. Follow the setup instructions
  3. Based on your priority guide template and using your current knowledge of HTML, create an HTML version of your resume.
  4. Use your current knowledge of CSS to add formatting/styles to your resume.
  5. Create a pull request for code review and final submission.
    • Include PR template and use best practices checklists for HTML and CSS to check your own work.

Requirements

  • Your assignment will be viewed at a resolution of 1200 x 800.
